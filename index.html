Hi! I use data analysis and visualization to tell stories. I'm proficient in tools like R, Python and SQL, but also know how to connect with sources and highlight their voices.

I'm graduating from the University of Maryland in May with degrees in journalism and information science.

I’m currently a managing editor at The Diamondback, UMD’s independent student newspaper, and I've interned with Advertising Specialty Institute through the Dow Jones News Fund data reporting program, Montgomery Community Media, Baltimore Magazine and the Howard Center for Investigative Journalism.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, listening to music and exploring museums. Explore my page to see my clips and some of the data visualizations, graphics and projects I've worked on.
